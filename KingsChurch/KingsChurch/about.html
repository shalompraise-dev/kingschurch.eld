Our Beliefs

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and the ultimate authority for faith and life.

We believe in one God who exists eternally in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

We believe Jesus is the Son of God, who lived a sinless life, died on the cross, and rose again for our salvation.

We believe that salvation is a gift of grace received through faith in Jesus Christ alone.
